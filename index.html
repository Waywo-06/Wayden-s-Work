PROJECTS & IDEAS

I worked with a group of fellow developers on a food app concept which helps identify whether a food item is safe to eat or not. The main goal of this app is to prevent people from contracting foodborne illnesses from food items they may purpose or consume. This app was brought up to help communties in South Africa struggling with foodborne illnesses and to decrease the rate at which it spread. Incorporating artificial intelligence was a key requirement for the app’s development,it allowed us to implement dynamic decision-making, personalized interactions, and real-time data processing all of which were critical to the app’s functionality.
